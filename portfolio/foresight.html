Foresight Statistics App

Graphic Design, Prototyping, Android Development (In Progress)

Foresight Statistics is a volleyball stats recording app for Android. Being a varsity volleyball player, statistics are a big part of success in higher level volleyball. Noticing that the current options are sufficient but lacking in quality UI/UX I set out to design and develop my own stats application.

The app began as a series of sketches, later digitized using Illustrator. In the mean time the logo and partial branding was developed. Moving from the early mockups, the app was partially laid out using Adobe Experience Design. Since then the app has been in development and remains in progress as a project.
