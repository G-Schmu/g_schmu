Daft YouTube

Graphic Design

Over the course of two years, I helped rebuild a Call of Duty YouTube channel. It originally started out with a logo redesign, upgrading from their previously poorly photoshopped design. With a new logo, we began forging a new identity for the channel as a whole.

While I was with the channel I helped produce various thumbnails, banner art, and other promotional and graphical items for the channel. It was a great experience overall and helped me continuously hone my design and style on various platforms.
