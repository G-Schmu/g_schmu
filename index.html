Lemon - Substance Tracker

  • App Development
  • Graphic Design

Lemon was a school project done as part of a course to look at the process of software development. With my previous experience in Android development I took on more of a project lead role with this app.

The main focus in developinng this app was Test Driven Development, Version Control, and Continuous Integration. The app idea itself is just a simple substance intake tracker allowing users to keep track of their alcohol and cannabis intake. This was implemented using simple Fragments and Firebase to store the data in the cloud.

I was also the main provider of the logo, color scheme, and UI design.
